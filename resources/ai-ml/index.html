AI & Machine Learning

Free and freemium tools for building, training, and deploying machine learning models.

  • 🚀

    Google Colab

    Free Jupyter notebook environment with GPU (Tesla T4/K80) support. Great for training and prototyping ML models.

  • 🏆

    Kaggle

    Data science platform with free GPU (30 hrs/week), TPU access, datasets, competitions, and community notebooks.

  • 🤖

    Hugging Face

    Platform for NLP models, datasets, and Spaces (free hosting for ML demos). Essential for AI/ML practitioners.

  • 📊

    CometML

    ML experiment tracking and MLOps platform. Free for individuals and academics. Track models, data, and pipelines.

  • 🤖

    Maxim AI

    End-to-end evaluation and observability platform for AI agents. Free for indie developers and small teams (3 seats).

  • 📚

    Deepnote

    Collaborative data science notebook (Jupyter-compatible). Free tier includes unlimited personal projects and basic machines.

  • 💻

    Datalore (JetBrains)

    Python notebooks by JetBrains with 10 GB storage and 120 hours runtime per month. Great for data analysis.

  • 🛰

    Replicate

    Run open-source ML models in the cloud via API. Free tier available for experimentation and prototyping.

  • 🎭

    Gradio

    Build and share ML demos with a Python library. Free hosting on Hugging Face Spaces.

  • 🚀

    Streamlit Community Cloud

    Deploy data apps and ML demos for free directly from your GitHub repo. Great for showcasing AI projects.

  • 📕

    Arize AI

    ML observability platform for monitoring model performance and detecting drift. Free for up to 2 models.

  • 🌐

    Azure Cognitive Services

    Pre-built AI APIs (Computer Vision, Translator, Face, Speech) with free tier including limited transactions.
