Design & Creative Resources

UI/UX design tools, graphic design platforms, fonts, icons, and other creative assets.

  • 🎨

    Figma

    Industry-standard UI/UX design tool. Free tier includes unlimited files, 3 projects, and real-time collaboration.

  • 🎨

    Canva

    Graphic design platform for social media, presentations, and branding. Free tier with thousands of templates.

  • 🗃

    Penpot

    Open-source design and prototyping tool. Free, browser-based alternative to Figma.

  • 🗑

    Remove.bg

    AI-powered background removal from images. Free for low-resolution images, API available.

  • 🗃

    drawDB

    Free and open-source online database diagram editor. No signup required for basic use.

  • 📝

    Google Fonts

    Free, open-source font library with 1,500+ families. Easy to embed in any web project.

  • 🎨

    Fontsource

    Self-host open-source fonts. Package manager approach to fonts for modern web development.

  • 💅

    Icons8

    Free icons, illustrations, and photos. Large library of design assets for web and mobile.

  • 🎨

    unDraw

    Open-source illustrations with a built-in color customization tool. Perfect for landing pages.

  • 🎨

    Humaaans

    Free, mix-and-match illustrations of people. Great for adding human elements to designs.

  • 🌈

    Coolors

    Color palette generator. Create, save, and export color schemes for your projects.

  • 📷

    Unsplash

    Free high-resolution stock photos. All images are free to use for commercial and non-commercial projects.
